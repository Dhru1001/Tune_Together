Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a collaboration project?

To start a collaboration project, go to the "Collaborations" page and click on the "Create Project" button. Fill in the project details, invite collaborators, and start working together!

Can I upload my own music tracks?

Yes, you can upload your own music tracks. Go to the "My Tracks" page and click on the "Upload Track" button. Follow the instructions to upload and share it with your collaborators.

How do I invite others to collaborate?

Go to the "Collaborations" page, select your project, and click on "Invite Collaborators". Enter their email addresses and send the invite.

Can I share files with my collaborators?

Yes, visit your project page and go to the "Files" section. Upload files and your collaborators can download them.

How do I track the progress of my project?

Use the project dashboard to monitor tasks, messages, and shared files. Analytics are also available to help track progress.

What if I have a problem or need help?

Head over to the Contact Us page and fill out the support form. Our team will assist you as soon as possible.
